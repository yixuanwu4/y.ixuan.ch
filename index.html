ABOUT ME

Hi! I am Yixuan Wu. I was born in Wuhan, China.

After completing my Bachelor's degree in 2018, I headed to the United Kingdom to pursue my Master's studies.

In 2019, I obtained my first master's degree in Psycholinguistics from the University of York.

Following that, I spent three years studying Computational Linguistics for another master's degree at the University of Tuebingen in Germany. During this time, I developed a strong interest in programming and gained knowledge in the field.

I have worked on various projects in Natural Language Processing, but currently, my focus has shifted towards web development and web design. My passion for programming continues to drive me to explore and undertake more projects in this dynamic and ever-changing field.
